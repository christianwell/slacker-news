People send me high-stakes messages about getting unbanned. Wanting to sound Serious and Professional, they turn to AI to rephrase and polish their message.

Don't. If you use AI, I will take you less seriously, not more. 

Sorry. It's not some kind of ideological holy war, I'm just being practical. I can't tell the difference between "Claude, here's my apology, make it classy", and "Claude, write my apology, and make it sincere."

I want to hear from *you*. I promise I don't care about your splling, you're grammar, or your mastery of the em dash--really. I just want to know that what you're saying is real. Imagine if Hack Club HQ used AI to write our communications with you.

I'm not alone. I know lots of people at HQ who feel this way, probably the majority, possibly everyone. Whether it's a ban appeal or a job application, let us talk to the real you.

*Postscript: there is an ongoing debate about the use of AI to anonymize #meta posts. This piece is not about that.*
